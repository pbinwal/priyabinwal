  With each passing day,
  I discover myself to be more of an appendage to the Universe.<br>
  <br>

  On some days I'm a wing that broke off a bird who was meant to take to the skies.<br>
  <br>

  On others I'm a limb that should have supported a friend but was never lent.<br>
  <br>

  And today I'm a milk-tooth, that though once indispensable, is now wobbly,<br>
  and will soon be replaced by someone very much like me— just stronger.
  </p>
